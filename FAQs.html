Frequently Asked Questions

What is Safe Harbour?

Safe Harbour is a non-profit organization based in Cape Town, South Africa, dedicated to providing support and resources to refugees and asylum seekers. Our mission is to offer a safe haven and assist individuals in their journey towards stability and integration.


How can I get involved with Safe Harbour?

There are several ways to get involved with Safe Harbour. You can make a donation, volunteer your time, or help spread the word about our cause. Visit our Contact Page for more details on how to contribute.


What types of support does Safe Harbour offer?

We offer a range of services including emergency shelter, legal aid, healthcare, and educational programs. Our goal is to provide comprehensive support to help refugees and asylum seekers rebuild their lives.


Who can receive assistance from Safe Harbour?

Assistance is available to all refugees and asylum seekers residing in Cape Town. We work to ensure that everyone in need receives the help and support they require regardless of their background.


Where is Safe Harbour located?

Our office is located at 1234 Peace Street, Cape Town, South Africa. Feel free to visit us during our office hours or contact us via email or phone for any inquiries.
