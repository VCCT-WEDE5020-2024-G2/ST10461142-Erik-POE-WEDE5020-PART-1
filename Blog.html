The Impact of our work


One of our proudest achievements is the sense of community we've fostered. Here at Safe Harbour, refugees and asylum seekers find not just services but also a network of people who care people who understand their journey and walk alongside them every step of the way. Our impact is measured in the stories of resilience and success that come from those we support. Each person who walks through our doors is not just another case; they are a part of our family. And together, we’re building a brighter, more inclusive future for all.

Stories of Hope and Resilience

Nyasha’s Journey to Education

Nyasha, a bright 14-year-old girl from Zimbabwe, arrived in Cape Town with her mother, fleeing political unrest. Struggling to find stability, they discovered Safe Harbour, which provided them with shelter and access to education. Nyasha, who had missed years of schooling, quickly caught up thanks to tutoring and a supportive community. Today, she is not only excelling in her studies but also dreams of becoming a teacher to help other children like her. Safe Harbour has given Nyasha the tools and confidence to build a brighter future for herself and her family.


Mbemba’s Path to Empowerment

Mbemba, a 17-year-old boy from the Democratic Republic of Congo, fled his war-torn country after losing his family. Arriving in Cape Town alone, he was lost and afraid until he found Safe Harbour. The organization provided him with legal assistance to obtain asylum and connected him with a mentorship program. Mbemba discovered his talent for carpentry and now works as an apprentice, with aspirations of starting his own business. Safe Harbour not only gave Mbemba a second chance at life but also a purpose and a path to independence.


Fatima’s Dream of Peace

Fatima, a 12-year-old girl from Palestine, escaped the conflict in her homeland with her family, seeking refuge in Cape Town. Traumatized by the violence she had witnessed, Fatima struggled to adjust to her new environment. Safe Harbour offered her and her family psychological support and a safe space to heal. Through art therapy, Fatima began to express her emotions and found comfort in drawing. Today, she is thriving in school, and her artwork is a powerful testament to her resilience. Safe Harbour helped Fatima find peace within herself and hope for a peaceful future.
